MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Between: DataPilot Inc. ("Provider")
And: GlobalSync Ltd. ("Customer")
Effective Date: February 1, 2025
Contract Term: 24 months (through January 31, 2027)

=== 1. SUBSCRIPTION TERMS ===

1.1 Plan: Enterprise
1.2 Base Fee: $1,499/month
1.3 Seat License: 18 seats at $100/seat/month = $1,800/month
1.4 Total Monthly Fee: $3,299/month
1.5 Annual Contract Value (ACV): $39,588
1.6 Billing Cycle: Annual (prepaid)
1.7 Payment Terms: Net 30 from invoice date

1.8 Included Features:
    - Unlimited SQL queries against connected databases
    - Document AI processing (up to 1,000 documents)
    - Analytics and chart generation
    - API access (10,000 requests/month)
    - SSO integration (SAML 2.0 / OIDC)
    - Audit logging
    - Dedicated CSM (Customer Success Manager)
    - Priority support (4-hour SLA for critical issues)

=== 2. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT ===

2.1 Uptime Commitment: 99.9% monthly uptime
2.2 Measurement: Calculated as ((total_minutes - downtime_minutes) / total_minutes) * 100
2.3 Exclusions: Scheduled maintenance (max 4 hours/month, with 48-hour notice)

2.4 Uptime Credits:
    - 99.0% - 99.9%: 10% credit on monthly fee
    - 95.0% - 99.0%: 25% credit on monthly fee
    - Below 95.0%: 50% credit on monthly fee

2.5 Response Time SLAs:
    - Critical (system down): 1 hour response, 4 hour resolution target
    - High (major feature broken): 4 hour response, 24 hour resolution target
    - Medium (minor issue): 8 hour response, 72 hour resolution target
    - Low (enhancement request): 24 hour response, best effort resolution

=== 3. DATA HANDLING ===

3.1 Data Ownership: Customer retains all ownership of data processed through the platform.
3.2 Data Location: United States (primary), Europe (backup)
3.3 Data Processing: Provider processes data solely for providing the subscribed services.
3.4 Data Deletion: Upon contract termination, Provider will delete all Customer data
    within 30 days and provide written confirmation.
3.5 Subprocessors: Groq Inc. (LLM inference), Google Cloud (fallback LLM)

=== 4. SECURITY ===

4.1 Provider maintains SOC 2 Type I compliance (Type II in progress).
4.2 All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
4.3 Provider will notify Customer of any data breach within 72 hours.
4.4 Annual penetration testing by independent third party.
4.5 Vulnerability remediation: Critical within 24 hours, High within 7 days.

=== 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ===

5.1 Provider retains all IP rights to the DataPilot platform.
5.2 Customer retains all IP rights to their data and any custom configurations.
5.3 Provider may use anonymized, aggregated usage data for product improvement.
5.4 No Customer data will be used to train AI/ML models without explicit consent.

=== 6. TERMINATION ===

6.1 Either party may terminate with 90 days written notice.
6.2 Customer may terminate immediately if Provider breaches SLA for 3 consecutive months.
6.3 Early termination fee: Remaining contract value at 50% discount.
6.4 Provider will provide data export in CSV/JSON format within 14 days of termination notice.

=== 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY ===

7.1 Provider's total liability shall not exceed 12 months of fees paid by Customer.
7.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
7.3 Exclusions: Gross negligence, willful misconduct, or breach of confidentiality.
